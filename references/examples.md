# Regression Examples

Use these examples when checking whether the skill is behaving correctly. They are not canned answers; they describe the expected reasoning shape and common traps.

## 量子能量项链靠谱吗？

Expected behavior:

- Decompose into "quantum energy affecting the body" and any measurable material claims such as radiation shielding or magnetic effect.
- If "quantum energy" has no standard scientific referent, mark that mechanism 🔴.
- Do not spend the answer on ridicule; explain that no valid mechanism or authoritative evidence supports the claim.

Common trap:

- Treating "quantum" as if it could plausibly improve health without a defined mechanism.

## 胶原蛋白口服液是智商税吗？

Expected behavior:

- Separate protein digestion/peptides, skin outcomes, dose/duration, and marketing claims such as "directly补到脸上".
- Human evidence may support small skin hydration/elasticity effects for specific hydrolyzed collagen products/doses, but broad beauty claims are often exaggerated.
- Likely verdict depends on intent: severe skin problem treatment is not supported; mild cosmetic improvement may be 🟡 if dose/product matches evidence.

Common trap:

- Saying "蛋白质都会被消化所以完全没用" without checking human trials and reviews.

## 奶瓶消毒柜值得买吗？

Expected behavior:

- Separate UV-C or heat/steam sterilization, drying/storage, and specific product execution.
- Established disinfection mechanisms can be 🟢 while brand execution may be ⚪/🟡 without specs or independent testing.
- Buying advice should distinguish high-risk infant contexts, convenience, and whether regular boiling/steam cleaning is cheaper enough.

Common trap:

- Demanding a brand-specific RCT and therefore incorrectly saying all evidence is insufficient.

## 益生菌真的能增强免疫力吗？

Expected behavior:

- Separate strain-specific evidence, target outcome, survival/dose, and vague "immune boosting" marketing.
- Stronger evidence may exist for narrow outcomes such as antibiotic-associated diarrhea with specific strains; broad daily immune enhancement in healthy people is often unsupported or exaggerated.
- Advice must be by intent and strain, not "all probiotics work" or "all probiotics are useless".

Common trap:

- Treating probiotics as one uniform intervention.

## 褪黑素助眠有用吗？

Expected behavior:

- Separate circadian rhythm timing, insomnia symptom relief, dose/timing, and safety/drug interaction context.
- Evidence is stronger for circadian rhythm problems and jet lag than for all-cause chronic insomnia.
- Include medical boundary for children, pregnancy, chronic disease, sedatives, anticoagulants, epilepsy, or persistent insomnia.

Common trap:

- Giving a supplement recommendation without safety caveats or current guideline/source verification.

## Generic Forward-Test Prompts

- "X 是智商税吗？我主要想知道该不该买。"
- "这个产品宣传能增强免疫力，靠谱吗？"
- "某品牌 <device> 说能杀菌/除甲醛/改善睡眠，值得买吗？"
- "吃 <supplement> 能预防 <condition> 吗？"
- "网上说 <folk remedy> 很有效，有没有科学依据？"
